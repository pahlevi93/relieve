About Relieve

Our Vision

Relieve believe that every human being has personal problems and need the support of others. Relieve have a goal to make all people in the world have peace of mind that can create a better human life instead of depression and despair.

User VS Reliever

User

Users are anonymous people who have personal problems and want to express and resolve with the Reliever.

Reliever

Reliever are psychology therapists , psychiatrists and people who have a background in psychology. Reliever listen to personal problems of the user and help resolve user problems online with Relieve. Everyone can signup as a Reliever by Login as Reliever.

ONLY SELECTED PEOPLE THAT FULFILL OUR REQUIREMENT CAN BE A RELIEVER.

The Logo Philosophy
